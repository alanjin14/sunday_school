Quick Recap from Last Week

From Scattered → Sending

Acts 8: Persecution scatters believers
→
They plant the Antioch church
Acts 13: Antioch sends Paul + Barnabas
→
Gospel goes to the ends of the earth

The persecuted became the senders. God writes the best comeback stories.

Key Idea 1

The Holy Spirit Initiates God's Mission

  • The church wasn't in a strategy meeting — they were worshiping and fasting
  • The Holy Spirit's fingerprints are everywhere in Acts (13:4, 13:9, 16:6-9)
  • God already has a plan — we just need to find our place in it
  • "God is always calling — are we responding?"

Two-Part Test

Are You Actually Listening?
Are You Actually Responding?

  • Listening: put down the 5x2 inch screen long enough to hear
  • Responding: don't ghost God when His call is uncomfortable
  • "Sometimes His calling is costly. That's the point of being a living sacrifice."

Key Idea 2

The Church Responds: Sender AND Sent

The Holy Spirit initiates. The Church responds — in two roles.

The Church as SENDER

Antioch gave THREE things:

  • 💰 Resources — Antioch was wealthy. They gave generously.
  • 💔 Their best people — Barnabas + Paul, no leftovers
  • 🙏 Prayer + encouragement — they backed their missionaries

ECC parallel: ~$500K/yr, 40+ long-term missionaries, 17 countries, 7 from ECC. Mission Fund needs YOUR earmarked giving.

The Church as SENT

Attributes of "the Sent":

🎯Missional — share with anyone
📅Available — God's agenda
🔄Flexible — drop your plans
✋Willing — say yes
💝Servant heart — selfless (like Paul: beaten, jailed, faithful)

Plot Twist

Paul used to be a persecutor.

He was part of the very persecution that scattered believers to Antioch — the church that's now sending him out.

"He is a chosen instrument of mine to carry my name before the Gentiles and kings…" — Acts 9:15

If God can use Paul, He can use you.

"For to me, to live is Christ and to die is gain." — Philippians 1:21

Paul fully embodied the surrendered life.

Key Idea 3

Your Mission in the Great Commission

  • All are called to the Great Commission
  • Not all are called to cross-cultural missions
  • Start local — but don't stop there
  • "The Great Commission is not the Great Suggestion." — Rick Warren

Demystifying "Calling"

Three Steps to Find Yours

Step 1
📖 LEARN
Posture of a student. Explore what God is doing. Take a class, browse joshuaproject.net, attend a missions conference.
Step 2
🧭 DISCERN
Look at: Passion · Gifting · Opportunity · Prayer · Wise counsel. What's God wired you for?
Step 3
✅ CONFIRM
Try it. Watch for the Holy Spirit and others to confirm. Pastor Steve Moy was already pastoring before he was ordained.

5 Discern Questions

Ask Yourself…

🔥Passion — Do I care?
🎁Gifting — Am I wired for it?
🚪Opportunity — Is a door opening?
🙏Prayer + Spirit — Am I asking?
👴Wise Counsel — What do mature believers see in me?

**This Week's Sunday School: Scattered, Not Shattered**

Dear Parents,

This week we studied **Acts 8:1-8** — how persecution scattered the early church, yet believers boldly preached wherever they went. Philip, an ordinary table server, brought the gospel to Samaria and "there was much joy in that city" (v. 8).

**Key verse:** *"For God gave us a spirit not of fear but of power and love and self-discipline."* — 2 Timothy 1:7

Ask your student: *"Where is your 'Samaria' — a place God might be sending you?"*

Blessings,
ECC Sunday School Team

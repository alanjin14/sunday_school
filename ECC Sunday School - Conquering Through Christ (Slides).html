Easter Sermon

Conquering Through Christ

  • The Question: What shall we say to these things? (v. 31a)
  • 5 Answers: Five rhetorical questions (vv. 31b-36)
  • Conclusion: God's love is the power that overcomes (vv. 37-39)

Key Idea 1

God Already Paid the Highest Price

  • If God gave up His own Son for us, why would He skip the easy stuff?
  • A man who buys a $500K boat doesn't leave it parked in his driveway
  • God paid the ultimate price to get you — He's NOT going to forget about you
  • "Has God forgotten me?" — Absolutely not. He's right there in your life.

Key Idea 2

Nothing Can Condemn You

  • "Not guilty" is NOT the same as "innocent"
  • Like a debt that's been paid by someone else — the debt is GONE
  • Jesus fully and completely paid our debt of sin. It is finished.
  • Satan wants you to wallow in guilt. God wants you to confess, get up, and keep walking.

Right Now, Jesus Is Praying for You

"Simon, Simon, behold, Satan has demanded to sift you like wheat, but I have prayed for you, that your faith will not fail." — Luke 22:31-32

Jesus prayed for Peter. He is praying for you and me right now at the right hand of God. TWO persons of the Trinity — Jesus AND the Holy Spirit — are praying for you.

Key Idea 3

We Are "Super Conquerors"

  • "Overwhelmingly conquer" = super conqueror
  • A super conqueror takes his enemies and makes them serve him
  • God takes what Satan uses against us and turns it for our good
  • We conquer not by our own strength but through Him who loved us

All Things Work Together for Good

💀 Death frees us from broken bodies → brings us to Jesus
🌱 Life gives us room to grow
👼 Angels serve us
⚔️ Trials strengthen our faith
🌍 Present things teach us only Christ satisfies
🔮 Future things are under God's control

Everything is made to serve God's saving, sanctifying love work in you.

"You are from God, little children, and have overcome them; because greater is He who is in you than he who is in the world." — 1 John 4:4

C.H. Spurgeon — A Real Super Conqueror

  • One of the 5 greatest preachers in history
  • At age 20, a prank caused a stampede — 7 died. He suffered lifelong depression
  • Also suffered severe gout — bedridden for days at a time
  • Yet he never gave up. Planted 200+ churches. Started a seminary.
  • He conquered by holding on to Christ and never letting go

"You don't have to be happy and clappy all the time to be faithful to Christ."

How Should We Respond?

🌅 Bring Easter into your daily life
📖 Love and serve God
🤝 Care for those God cares for

Difficulties are not just burdens — they are opportunities to look to God.
Easter is resurrection power for living TODAY.
